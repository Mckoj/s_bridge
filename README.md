S-bridge is a PERN stack project 
This project follows a three tier user architecture; students , recruiters and Universities
It seeks to bridge the gap between difficulty in landing good intership opportunities for university student 
that are relevamt to their program of study to increase proficiency in what they are learning
It removes the bias and unfairness in the process of getting internships for qualified and willing students due to
nepotism, and networking that makes it difficult for the underprivileged students
by allowing university institutions to liase with companies to recruit students for training 

In this project , students can apply for internships based on their interest and most especially their program of study
Universities would now get this internship available to the student through their partnering companies.
Universities can track the progress of each student during the internship to ensure that there is a growth in the student
